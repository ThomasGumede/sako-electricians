Our Quality Services

Electrical installation

At Sako electrians, we specialize in providing top-notch new electrical installations for various properties. Our team of trained electrians ensures that every installation is carried out efficiently and safely, adhering to all relevant regulations and standards

Electrical Maintanance

We understand the importance of keeping your electrical systems in optimal condition. Our comprehensive electrical maintanance services help prevent potential issues, ensuring the smooth operation of your electrical infrastructure. From routine inspections to proactive repairs, we've got you covered.

Fault-finding & Repairs

Electrical faults can disrupt your daily activities & pose safety hazards. Our skilled electricians specialize in identifying & fixing electrical faults promptly & accurately. Using advanced diagnostic tools & techniques, we pipnpoint the root cause of the issue & implement effective solutions to restore functionality & safety.

Electrical Reviews

Regular electrical reviews are essential for identifying potential hazards and ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements. Our thorough electrical review services provide comprehensive assessments of your electrical systems, highlighting areas for improvement and recommending appropriate solutions. With our proactive approach, you can have peace of mind knowing that your property is safe and compliant.

Electrical Consulting

Navigating the complexities of electrical systems can be challenging. That's where our electrical consulting services come in. Whether you're planning a new construction project or upgrading your existing electrical infrastructure, our experienced consultants provide expert guidance and recommendations to ensure optimal performance, efficiency, and safety.

Electrical Rewiring

Outdated or faulty wiring can compromise the safety and efficiency of your property's electrical system. Our professional rewiring services help upgrade and modernize your wiring infrastructure, enhancing safety, reliability, and energy efficiency. We utilize industry-leading materials and techniques to deliver durable and long-lasting results.
